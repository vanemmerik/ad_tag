GAM Ad Tag Validation Tool

Inspect a Google Ad Manager (GAM 360) video & audio ad tag.
Each parameter is broken out and evaluated against the detected serving context (client-side / CSAI vs server-side / SSAI).

Retain tag ⓘKeeps your last inspected tag saved in this browser, so it is restored the next time you open the tool. ⓘCopies a shareable link to this inspection to your clipboard - open it to reload the tool with this tag.
